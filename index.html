HAPPINESS

“Happier than Ever”

Joy is like a warm sunrise after a long night, filling the heart with light and energy. It dances in laughter, sparkles in kind gestures, and shines in the simple moments of life—a shared smile, a song playing on the radio, the feeling of the sun on your skin. Joy is not just found in grand achievements but in the little things that make life beautiful. It’s the embrace of a loved one, the excitement of new adventures, and the comfort of cherished memories. No matter the circumstances, joy is always there, waiting to be embraced.

FEAR

“When we all fall asleep, where do we go ?”

Fear is a natural emotion, essential for survival. The fear of surprise is particularly intense—a sudden noise or an unexpected presence can make us jump, revealing our instinct to stay alert. Likewise, the feeling of being watched creates a deep, uneasy sensation. This discomfort likely comes from an ancient instinct, reminding us that an unseen gaze might signal a hidden threat.

LOVE

“I’ll love you ‘til the day that I die”

Every moment spent with you feels like a beautiful dream come true. Your smile lights up even the darkest days, and your presence fills my heart with warmth and happiness. I never knew love could be so pure and deep, but with you, I've found a love that feels eternal. You're not just the love of my life; you're my best friend, my confidant, and the person I want to spend every moment with, forever.
